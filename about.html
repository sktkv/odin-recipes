What is this website?
This website is an entry-point into sustainable gardening and consumption practices. In the Foraging section, you will find an index of some wild vegetables ('soppus' in Kannada) commonly seen around Bengaluru. Make sure you check out 'Chasing Soppu', linked in the source here.
In the days to come, we hope to create a database of easily growing plants in the city with information from local gardeners and include a forum for collaboration.
Watch this space for more!
Why an organic life?
People take to a more organic life for several reasons. For some, organic gardening allows them to take a moment's rest from the bustle of their urban existence. Some people enjoy the healthy lifestyle that comes with it: the walking by lakes in search of foragable leaves, the hard work in managing a garden, and the satisfaction of eating food that's low-carbon-footprint and pesticide-free!

Everyone, eventually, finds their own motivation. What are your reasons for sustainability?
